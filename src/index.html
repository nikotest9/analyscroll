Product A Highlights

This interactive article is a specially produced summary by BARC of the headline results for Product A. It contains just a selection of the headline findings for Product A. It does not show all the KPI results and focuses mainly on the positive findings for Product A. For more information on the survey, visit The BI Survey website.

BARC comment

This year again Product A underpins its status as a leader in business benefits in three of its peer groups. Customers mainly benefit from faster and more accurate reporting, analysis and planning, which leads to improved data quality, better business decisions and improved employee satisfaction.

Product A has maintained its ranking for business benefits from last year, creating good value for its customers and scoring consistently high ratings in business benefits in The BI Survey. Customers clearly benefit from having a flexible, integrated CPM platform for planning, simulation, strategy implementation (balanced scorecard) and project portfolio management.

BARC comment

Product A has topped the ‘Large enterprise BI platforms’ peer group for mobile BI use for the last seven years. Its score of 8.1/10 is well clear of The BI Survey average of 4.4/10. Product A was one of the first BI and analytics vendors to focus strongly on mobile BI. The vendor introduced a framework for building individual and customized mobile applications.

Not only do these apps serve typical information needs, but many customers have also built operational mobile applications which make use of further platform services such as Transaction Services to write back operational data and therefore support business processes.

BARC comment

Customers are satisfied and vote Product A as the number one for sales/purchasing experience in the ‘Large international BI vendors’ peer group. In particular, the overall rating of product evaluation and contract negotiation, timely and thorough response to product-related and technical questions, general behavior and the ability to understand organizations’ needs are highlighted by customers.

In a highly competitive market like the BI and CPM software market in the Americas region (Product A's home market), a highly professional sales organization is essential for survival and to constantly win new customers.

BARC's Summary on Product A's Highlights

Outstanding price-performance ratio paired with strong performance, vendor and product satisfaction convinces customers. Customers can greatly benefit from outperform in terms of faster and more accurate reporting, analysis or planning, overall leading to improved data quality, better business decisions and improved employee satisfaction. KPI Explanation
KPI Short explanation
Business benefits This KPI is based on the achievement level of a variety of business benefits.
Project success This KPI is based on the implementation satisfaction level and the frequency of projects completed on time and on budget.
Project length This KPI is based on how quickly the product is implemented.
Business value The KPI business value combines the ‘Business benefits’, ‘Project success’ and ‘Project length’ KPIs.
Price-to-value This KPI is based on how users rate their BI tool in terms of price-to-value.
Recommendation This KPI is based on how many users would recommend the product to others.
Vendor support This KPI measures user satisfaction with the level of vendor support provided for the product.
Implementer support This KPI measures user satisfaction with the level of the implementer’s support for the product.
Product satisfaction This KPI is based on the frequency of problems encountered with the product.
Customer satisfaction The KPI customer satisfaction combines the ‘Peice-to-value’, ‘Recommendation’, ‘Product satisfaction’, ‘Vendor support’ and ‘Implementer support’ KPIs.
Self-service This KPI is based on how many sites currently use self-service features with their BI tool.
Flexibility for the user This KPI is based on how often the product was chosen for its flexibility, and on the frequency of complaints about user flexibility post-implementation.
Ease of use This KPI is based on how often the product was chosen for its ease of use, and on the level of complaints about ease of use post-implementation.
Sales experience This KPI is based on how respondents rate the sales/purchasing experience with the vendor.
Data volume This KPI is based on the median volume of data in databases used with the BI tool.
Query performance This KPI is based on how quickly queries respond (adjusted by data volume).
Performance satisfaction This KPI measures the frequency of complaints about the system’s performance.
Customer experience The KPI customer satisfaction combines the ‘Ease of use’, ‘Self-service’, ‘Performance satisfaction’, ‘Flexibility for the user’, ‘Data volume’, ‘Query performance’ and ‘Sales experience‘ KPIs.
Cloud BI This KPI is based on how many sites currently use the product in a cloud environment.
Mobile BI This KPI is based on how many sites currently use mobile BI with their BI tool.
Operational BI This KPI is based on how many sites currently use real-time data from transactional systems with their BI tool.
Visual design standards This KPI is based on how many sites currently use visual design standards with their BI tool.
Location intelligence This KPI is based on how many sites currently use spatial/location analysis with their BI tool.
Visual analysis This KPI is based on how many sites currently use visual analysis with their BI tool.
Embedded BI This KPI is based on how many survey respondents use embedded BI.
Innovation The KPI innovation combines the ‘Embedded BI‘, ‘Cloud BI’, ‘Visual analysis‘, ‘Mobile BI’, ‘Operational BI‘, ‘Location intelligence‘ and ‘Visual design standards’ KPIs to measure the product’s level of innovation.
Considered for purchase This KPI is based on whether respondents considered purchasing the product.
Competitive win rate This KPI is based on the percentage of wins in competitive evaluations.
Competitiveness The KPI competitiveness combines the ‘Considered for purchase’ and ‘Competitive win rate’ KPIs.
